Workshops at Cake Atelier Copenhagen

What we do

Cake Atelier workshops are designed as a creative journey — from learning the basics, to practicing with confidence, to gradually exploring your own style.

Sessions usually take place in my home studio in Copenhagen, with occasional collaborations hosted in other selected venues.

These are hobby-style experiences, shaped by real practice and curiosity rather than formal certification. Everything I share comes from my own path — the things I would have loved to know when I was starting out.

If you’re curious, hands-on, or simply enjoy creating with others, you’ll feel at home here.

Your Cake Atelier Journey

  • Level 1. Beginner Buttercream Basics Learn the fundamentals
  • Level 2. Practice Cake Atelier Club Practice, experiment, improve, socialize
  • Level 3. Advanced Creative Buttercream Style-focused & themed workshops
  • Creative Event Cake Painting & Coffee Relaxed, social gathering

All experiences are hobby-based and non-certified.
