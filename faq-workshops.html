Booking & Participation

Do I need experience to join a workshop?

No experience is needed.

Workshops are designed for beginners and curious creatives and guided step by step.

What’s included in a workshop?

Unless stated otherwise, workshops include:

  • All tools and materials
  • A cake to decorate
  • Guidance and techniques
  • Your finished cake to take home

Food and drinks depend on the venue and are specified per event.

Are workshops suitable for children?

Workshops are designed for adults unless otherwise stated.

Tickets & Payment

How do I book a workshop?

Workshop spots are booked online or via direct registration links.

Your spot is secured only once full payment is received.

Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?

Yes. You may transfer your spot to another person.

Please notify me in advance with the new participant’s details.

Are workshop tickets refundable?

Workshop tickets are non-refundable.

This is due to limited capacity, preparation, and material costs.

Cancellations

What happens if I can’t attend?

If you cannot attend:

  • You may transfer your ticket to someone else
  • No refunds are issued
What if Cake Atelier cancels a workshop?

If a workshop is cancelled by Cake Atelier:

  • You will receive a full refund or credit, at your choice

Photos & Media

Are photos or videos taken during workshops?

Yes, photos or short videos may be taken during workshops for:

  • Website
  • Social media
  • Marketing purposes

Participation implies consent.

If you prefer not to appear, please let me know before or during the event — no problem at all.

Health & Safety

Are there any requirements to participate?
  • Workshops involve standing, tools, and food handling
  • Closed-toe shoes are recommended
  • Please inform me of relevant allergies in advance

Participation is at your own responsibility.

Private & Corporate Workshops

Can workshops be booked privately?

Yes. Workshops can be booked for:

  • Private groups
  • Team building
  • Birthdays
  • Bridal showers

Dates, formats, and pricing are arranged individually.

Liability

Cake Atelier Copenhagen is not responsible for:

  • Injuries caused by misuse of tools
  • Allergic reactions where ingredients were disclosed
  • Personal belongings during workshops
