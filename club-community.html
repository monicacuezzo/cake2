Cake Atelier Community

A place for cake lovers to gather, share, and grow — together.

The Cake Atelier Community is where baking becomes social.

A space for people who love cakes, decorating, and the creative process — whether you bake every weekend or you’re just starting out.

This is not about perfection.

It’s about showing up, learning, and enjoying the process.

A cozy online space

What is the Cake Atelier Community?

At the heart of the community is a private Facebook group — a cozy online space where cake lovers come together.

What happens inside

Inside the group, you’re invited to:

Share what you’re baking or decorating

Post progress photos, ideas, and experiments

Ask questions (big or small)

Learn from others and support each other along the way

I’m there too — as the admin and fellow baker — answering questions, sharing tips, and keeping the space warm, respectful, and encouraging.

Think of it as a shared kitchen table, open to everyone who loves cake.

Beyond the screen

From Online to Real Life

Sometimes, it’s nice to step away from the screen.

That’s why the Cake Atelier Community also comes together in real life — through relaxed gatherings and meet-ups during the year, especially in the summer.

We meet in parks or informal spaces, bring something we’ve baked, and share it with like-minded people.

Some gatherings are free, others are ticketed depending on the format — but they all keep the same spirit: easy, social, creative, and welcoming.

You come as you are. You bring what you’ve baked. That’s enough.

Come as you are

Is This for You?

This community might be for you if:

  • You enjoy baking and decorating cakes
  • You like learning by doing (even when things don’t turn out perfectly)
  • You’re curious, creative, and enjoy helping and sharing with others
  • You’re looking for a relaxed, supportive baking community

You don’t need to be experienced.

You don’t need fancy tools.

You just need a love for cake.

How it may grow

Looking Ahead

The Cake Atelier Community is designed to grow organically.

In the future, this space may expand into:

  • Baking retreats
  • Themed creative weekends
  • Special workshops and experiences
  • Membership-based formats and collaborations

But at its heart, it will always be about people, cake, and the joy of creating together.
