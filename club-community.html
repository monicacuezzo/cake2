Cake Atelier Community

A space for the curious, the creative, and the cake-obsessed.

The Cake Atelier Community is where baking becomes social.

A space for people who love cakes, decorating, and the creative process — whether you bake every weekend or you’re just starting out.

By joining the community, you become part of an inner circle that gets early access, special invitations, and a closer connection to everything happening behind the scenes.

Member benefits

What it means to be a Member:

  • Early Access & Member Pricing: Be the first to know about new workshops dates, events, and collaborations — before they are released publicly. Community members receive access to selected sessions at a reduced price.
  • The Inner Circle Events: Beyond the standard workshops, I host small, relaxed gatherings and seasonal creative sessions reserved exclusively for this group.
  • A Shared Table: Access to our private digital space. A private Facebook group where you can share your bakes, ask questions, exchange ideas, and connect with others who enjoy the process as much as you do. I’ll also be there — sharing insights, recipes, and answering questions along the way.
  • Community Perks & Giveaways: I love working with other makers. From time to time, I collaborate with other creatives and businesses to offer tickets, experiences, or gifts — shared exclusively within the community.
  • Future Experiences: As the community grows, so will the experiences — think creative retreats and baking-focused trips. You’ll be the first to help me shape those dreams.

Come as you are

Is This for You?

This community might be for you if:

  • You enjoy baking and decorating cakes
  • You like learning by doing (even when things don’t turn out perfectly)
  • You’re curious, creative, and enjoy helping and sharing with others
  • You’re looking for a relaxed, supportive baking community

You don’t need to be experienced.

You don’t need fancy tools.

You just need a love for cake.

Stay in the loop

How to Join

I value your inbox as much as my own. Joining is free, and it means signing up to receive occasional emails (2–3 per month), where I share:

  • New workshop releases (before public launch)
  • Community-only offers and invitations
  • Updates, ideas, and behind-the-scenes moments

You can unsubscribe at any time.
