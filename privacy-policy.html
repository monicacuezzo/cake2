This Privacy Policy explains how Cake Atelier Copenhagen collects, uses, and protects your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Data Controller

Cake Atelier Copenhagen

Owner: Ornella Vanina Acciarri

CVR: 45259846

Location: Amager, Copenhagen, Denmark

Email: admin@cakeateliercph.dk

Cake Atelier Copenhagen is the data controller responsible for your personal data.

What Data We Collect

We may collect and process the following personal data:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Order details (cake preferences, event information)
  • Workshop booking details
  • Communication via email, forms, or social media
  • Payment-related information (processed via third-party providers)

How We Use Your Data

Your personal data is used to:

  • Process and manage orders and workshop bookings
  • Communicate with you regarding your request
  • Send confirmations, reminders, and updates
  • Handle customer service inquiries
  • Comply with legal obligations (e.g. accounting)

We do not sell or share your personal data for marketing purposes.

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

  • Contractual necessity (to fulfil your order or booking)
  • Legal obligations (e.g. bookkeeping requirements)
  • Legitimate interest (customer communication and service management)
  • Consent (for marketing or use of images, where applicable)

Data Sharing

Your data may be shared with trusted third parties where necessary:

  • Payment providers (e.g. MobilePay, banks)
  • Booking platforms (if applicable)
  • Accounting software (e.g. Dinero)

These providers process your data in accordance with GDPR.

Data Retention

Personal data is retained only as long as necessary:

  • Accounting and invoice data: up to 5 years (Danish legal requirement)
  • Customer communication: as needed for service purposes
  • Marketing consent: until withdrawn

Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise your rights, please contact:

hello@cakeateliercph.dk

Complaints

If you wish to file a complaint regarding how your data is handled, you can contact:

Datatilsynet

Website: www.datatilsynet.dk

Photos & Media

Photos and videos may be taken during workshops for marketing purposes.

By participating, you consent to this use.

If you prefer not to appear, please inform us at the beginning of the session.

Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

Cookies

This website may use basic cookies to improve functionality and user experience.

If tracking or analytics tools are used, a cookie banner will be provided where required.

Changes to This Policy

Cake Atelier Copenhagen reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy when necessary.
