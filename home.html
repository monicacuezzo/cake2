CAKE ATELIER Copenhagen is a small, independent cake studio based in Copenhagen.

What we do

We create custom cakes for birthdays, intimate weddings, office celebrations, and meaningful gatherings — alongside fun, beginner-friendly cake decorating workshops and events.

Every cake is made to order, with a focus on flavour balance and artistic designs with swiss meringue buttercream and ganache. They are made to represent the soul of the moment they were created for and meant to be shared around the table with love.

Meet Ornella, the Heart Behind Cake Atelier

What began as a journey of self-discovery blossomed into Cake Atelier CPH — a place where passion for art and love of baking come to life.
