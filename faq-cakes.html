Ordering

How far in advance should I order a cake?

I recommend ordering 5–7 days in advance, depending on availability. For custom designs, weekends, or busy seasons, earlier booking is strongly advised.

Last-minute orders may be possible but are not guaranteed.

How do I place an order?

Orders can be placed via:

  • The website order form
  • Email or Instagram (confirmation will still follow by email)

An order is confirmed only once full payment is received.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes. Full payment upfront is required to secure your order and date.

Can I make changes after confirming my order?

Minor changes (such as color tones or short text adjustments) may be possible up to 72 hours before pickup, depending on production status.

Changes to:

  • Cake size
  • Flavor
  • Design concept

are generally not possible once production has started.

Do you offer tastings?

At this time, tastings are not offered as a standard service.

Flavor descriptions are provided to help you choose confidently.

Cakes & Design

Will my cake look exactly like the reference photo?

Each cake is handmade and unique.

Reference images are used as inspiration, not exact replicas.

Small variations in color, texture, or finish are part of the artisanal process.

How many servings does each cake size provide?

Serving sizes are estimates based on standard portions.

Final portions depend on how the cake is cut.

Ingredients & Allergies

Do you accommodate allergies or dietary restrictions?

Please note:

  • Cakes may contain or come into contact with gluten, dairy, eggs, nuts, soy, and alcohol
  • The kitchen is not allergen-free

While I can sometimes adapt flavors, I cannot guarantee the absence of allergens.

If allergies are critical, please contact me before ordering.

Pickup & Transport

Do you offer delivery?

Currently, cakes are pickup only, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

Pickup details (date, time, and address) are confirmed in advance.

What happens after pickup?

Once collected, the responsibility for:

  • Transport
  • Storage
  • Handling

passes to the customer.

Cake Atelier Copenhagen is not responsible for damage occurring after pickup.

What if I’m late for pickup?

Please arrive on time.

If delayed, notify me as soon as possible.

Quality and safe storage cannot be guaranteed for significantly delayed pickups.

Cancellations & Refunds — Cakes

What is your cancellation and refund policy?

Because cakes are custom-made:

  • More than 7 days before pickup: Partial refund may be offered, excluding costs already incurred
  • Less than 7 days before pickup: No refund
  • Same-day cancellation or no-show: No refund
