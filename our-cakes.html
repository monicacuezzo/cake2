Our Cakes

What’s Your Cake Mood?

At Cake Atelier Copenhagen, cakes are designed around how you want to experience them.

We offer two distinct cake styles — roll cakes and traditional layered cakes — each suited to different occasions, tastes, and moments.

Whether you’re after a light dessert to share or a rich cake that anchors the celebration, there’s a clear choice.

Roll Cakes Cakes

ROLL CAKES

Light sponge cakes with whipped mascarpone or ganache

Roll cakes are made with a soft chiffon-style sponge, rolled with whipped mascarpone cream and/or light ganache, finished with flavour accents such as fruit, citrus, tea, or nuts.

They are airy, balanced, and less sweet, closer to a plated dessert than a classic celebration cake. There is no buttercream, and decoration remains minimal and natural — letting flavour lead.

Best for

  • Casual celebrations and informal gatherings
  • Dinners, office sharing & weekday treats
  • Those who prefer lighter desserts
  • One standard size (serves approx. 6–10 people)

TRADITIONAL CAKES

Layered celebration cakes with buttercream

Traditional cakes are rich, layered cakes made with soft sponge, generous fillings, and finished with Swiss meringue buttercream.

They are designed to be celebration centrepieces— customizable in flavour, size, and design.Decoration ranges from clean and minimal to expressive, abstract, and artistic, inspired by colour, texture, and movement.

Best for

  • Birthdays & milestone celebrations
  • Small weddings & intimate events
  • When the cake is part of the visual experience

Cake sizes & structure

structure

All traditional cakes share the same structure:

  • 1 Tier
  • Approx. height 10 - 13 cm
  • Three layers of sponge
  • Two layers of filling
