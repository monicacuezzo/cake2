Cake Atelier workshops

Level 1. Buttercream Basics

Intro to Buttercream Cake Decorating

The perfect starting point if you’re new to cake decorating.

This 3-4 hour beginner workshop focuses on buttercream handling, smooth finishes, and basic piping, giving you a solid foundation to build on for future creations. The atmosphere is fun and supportive, with plenty of time to practice and ask questions.

Details

  • Duration: 3-4 hours
  • Group size: 4 to 30 people
  • Level: Beginner
  • Cake size: 4-inch (10 cm)

What’s included

  • One individual cake to decorate and take home
  • All tools, buttercream, colors & materials
  • Coffee, tea & light snacks
  • Follow-up PDF material and recipes

Price

from 850 DKK (Incl. VAT)

Level 2. Cake Atelier Club

Practice · Share · Improve

Cake Atelier Club is a guided practice space for anyone with some prior experience who wants time to decorate, experiment, have fun, and grow — together.

This is not a structured workshop, but a calm, community-focused session. You’ll work on your own cake, revisit techniques, ask questions, and receive feedback when needed — while sharing time and experiences with others like you.

Details

  • Duration: 2–2.5 hours
  • Group size: 4 to 20 people
  • Level: Some experience recommended
  • Cake size: 4-inch (10 cm)

What’s included

  • Cake, buttercream & materials
  • Use of all tools
  • Coffee, tea & light snacks
  • Guidance and feedback throughout

Pricing

From 670 DKK (Incl. VAT)

Level 3. Creative Buttercream

Style-Focused & Themed Advanced Workshops

Creative Buttercream is an umbrella for rotating intermediate to advanced workshops, each focused on a specific style or aesthetic.

These sessions are for those who already feel confident with buttercream basics and want to explore design, composition, texture, and color harmony in more depth. Themes change over time, while the format stays consistent.

Details

  • Duration: 3-4 hours
  • Group size: 4 to 30 people
  • Level: Intermediate / Advanced
  • Cake size: 6-inch (15 cm)

What’s included

  • One 6-inch cake
  • All tools, buttercream, colors & materials
  • Coffee, tea & light snacks
  • Follow-up PDF material and recipes

Example themes

  • Abstract petals & textures
  • Vintage piping
  • Botanical cakes with fresh flowers
  • Palette knife designs (realistic flowers)

Price

From 990 DKK (Incl. VAT)

(depending on theme & materials)

Event: Cake Painting & Coffee

Creative Gathering

Cake Painting & Coffee is a relaxed creative event.

We begin with a gentle introduction to painting with buttercream using palette knives and brushes. From there, the session becomes freestyle, social and fun— focused on creativity, conversation, and enjoying the process.

Details

  • Duration: 1.5 hours
  • Group size: 4 to 30 people
  • Level: Open to all

What’s included

  • Square cake portion (10cm × 10 cm)
  • Buttercream & tools
  • Coffee, tea & light snacks

Price

From 590 DKK (Incl. VAT)
